§2 Booking & deposit
1. Booking & Deposit

1.1 All studio bookings require a non-refundable 50% deposit paid online at the time of booking to secure your time slot.

1.2 The remaining 50% balance is due upon arrival at the studio before your session begins.

1.3 Your slot is not confirmed until the deposit payment is successfully processed. Unpaid bookings are automatically released after 15 minutes.

1.4 Booking confirmation and session details will be sent to your provided contact (WhatsApp, Telegram, or email as selected).

1.5 Prices are quoted in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) and include applicable taxes.

§3 Cancellation & rescheduling
2. Cancellation & Rescheduling

2.1 Rescheduling: Clients may reschedule their booking once at no charge, provided at least 48 hours' notice is given before the original session time. Rescheduling requests must be submitted via WhatsApp or Telegram to our studio team.

2.2 A second reschedule request will incur a rescheduling fee of RM 30.

2.3 Cancellations: The 50% deposit is non-refundable. If you cancel your booking, the deposit will not be returned regardless of notice period.

2.4 If CinnamonFoto Studio cancels your booking for any reason within our control, a full refund of the deposit will be issued within 5 working days.

2.5 No-shows (failure to arrive within 30 minutes of your session start time without prior notice) forfeit the deposit and the session slot.

§4 Session conduct
3. Session Conduct

3.1 Sessions begin at the booked start time. Clients who arrive late will not receive an extension — your session ends at the original scheduled end time to respect subsequent bookings.

3.2 The studio is a professional workspace. Clients are expected to treat equipment, backdrops, and props with care. Damage to studio property may result in additional charges.

3.3 Young children must be supervised at all times. Eating and drinking is restricted to the waiting area only.

3.4 The studio team reserves the right to pause or end a session if conduct is deemed inappropriate or poses a safety risk.

3.5 The number of people per session is subject to the package limits. Additional guests beyond the included count may affect session flow and quality.

§5 Photo & video usage rights

4.1 All photos and videos produced during your session remain the intellectual property of CinnamonFoto. You are granted a personal, non-commercial licence to use, print, and share your delivered files.

4.2 Commercial use of delivered photos/videos requires prior written permission and a separate licensing agreement.

4.3 CinnamonFoto reserves the right to use session photos in our portfolio, website, and social media for promotional purposes. If you prefer your photos not to be shared publicly, please indicate this in your booking notes — we will honour this request.

4.4 Edited files will be delivered within the timeframe specified for your package. Exact delivery dates are targets, not guarantees, and may vary during peak Raya season.

§6 Data & privacy
5. Data & Privacy

5.1 We collect personal data (name, phone number, email address) solely to process and manage your booking.

5.2 If you opt-in to Telegram notifications, your Telegram user ID will be stored for the purpose of sending booking confirmation and reminder messages only.

5.3 We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties outside of payment processing partners.

5.4 Booking data is retained for up to 2 years for accounting purposes, then securely deleted. You may request early deletion by contacting us directly.

5.5 For full details, see our Privacy Policy and Data Retention Notice.

§7 Liability
6. Liability

6.1 CinnamonFoto Studio is not liable for personal injury or loss of personal belongings within the studio premises.

6.2 In the unlikely event of equipment failure or technical issues that prevent delivery of your photos, our liability is limited to a refund of the deposit or an equivalent replacement session.

6.3 Force majeure events (natural disasters, power outages, government directives) are beyond our control. We will contact you promptly and offer a full reschedule at no charge.
