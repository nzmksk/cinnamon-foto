How are edited photos delivered?
All edited photos are delivered via a private online gallery link. You can download full-resolution JPEGs directly. The link is valid for 60 days. RAW files are included in Classic and Premium packages.
What happens if my session runs over time?
We'll give you a 5-minute warning before your time is up. If you'd like to continue, additional time can be purchased on the spot (subject to the next booking's schedule). We recommend adding time at checkout to guarantee availability.
