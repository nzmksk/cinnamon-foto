§2 Founder / brand story
Our story
Started in Sabah, built on authentic storytelling.

[Founder name] started CinnamonFoto with a single belief: that the best photos happen when people forget they're being photographed. Every wedding, every Raya session, every graduation portrait — we approach it as a documentary, not a production.

Based in Sabah, we grew up knowing these roads, these venues, and these cultural nuances. That local knowledge translates directly into better work — we know where the light falls in the late afternoon at a Keningau garden, and we know how to put families at ease in the moments between poses.

CinnamonFoto covers all of Sabah, from Kota Kinabalu to Tawau, with a style that is vibrant but never garish, emotional but never sentimental, and always honest.
